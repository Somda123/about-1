ABOUT

Book Details:

When a user selects a book from the search results, the app provides detailed information about the book. This includes the book's title, author, publication date, description, page count, and genre. Additionally, users can view the book's cover image and other relevant metadata.

Author Details:

The app also allows users to explore more about the authors. By clicking on an author's name, users can see a list of other books written by the same author, providing a deeper dive into their works and a chance to discover more related literature.

Genre Filtering:

Users can filter books by genre, helping them to easily navigate and find books that fit their interests. The app will categorize books into various genres based on the data provided by the Google Books API.
